GIMO

Gaussian International mathematical Olympiad(GIMO) is an IMO Level and format contest. The contest will be held on June 2021. For more information scroll down. Registration can be done over here on AoPS as a pre registration though for registration, maybe other process will be initiated later.

GIMO (Gaussian International Mathematical Olympiad)

It is an IMO level contest with the usual conditions and pattern same that of a IMO contest. It will be released on two days of three problems each and Day 1 and 2 will be released on a gap of 4 days where the submission deadline for day 1 will be 4 days and after submission deadline closed and then day 2 is released and you will be given again 4 days of time for the submission. Registration will start within this week.

General Information
  • Each problem is worth 7 points
  • Please use wisely the time since literally you have 23 hours for each day to workout with the problems though the submission window is open for 4 days including the day of release.
  • Contest Format is individual
  • A geometry problem solution without a diagram will result in a one point loss irrespective of its solution.
  • Time of release of Day 1 and 2 has been said at the end in a table.
  • Solutions and rubrics will be published on 15th June 2021
  • Results will be released on 26th June 2021.
