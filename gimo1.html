GIMO (Gaussian International Mathematical Olympiad)

It is an IMO level contest with the usual conditions and pattern same that of a IMO contest.

General Information

    Gaussian International Mathematical Olympiad is an MO style mathematical contest for individuals of 6 problems of 7 points each , where you will be surprisingly given three days to work out with the problems for each day and submit them after that the submission window will close. To make it more clear the problems will be released on two separate days with three problems on each day. So you will get a total of 6 days to work out with 6 problems. Be sure to show full reasoning that is detailed solution for each problem. Each problem is worth 7 points
  • Please use wisely the time since literally you have 3 days for each day to workout with the problems.

    • Contest Format is individual.

    A geometry problem solution without a diagram will result in a one point loss irrespective of its solution.

    Time of release of Day 1 and 2 has been said at the end in a table.

    Solutions and rubrics will be published on 15th June 2021

    Results will be released on 26th June 2021.
